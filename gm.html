WHAT IS BLUEQ?

BlueQ is an online platform that allows you to upload and manage qualifications and accreditations such as Working with Children Checks. AFL Goulburn Murray is using BlueQ so coaches, trainers and other club volunteers can easily manage this important information online meaning less time is spent on paperwork. Ensuring that everyone is appropriately qualified means we can provide a safe environment for all involved.

BENEFITS FOR CLUBS & Volunteers

  • Visibility & Compliance

    Clubs have visibility over their staff and volunteers to see who is and isn’t compliant

  • Verification

    Free and instant verification of qualifications like Working With Children Checks

  • Individual Accounts

    Staff and volunteers have their own account to manage their qualifications and will receive notifications when a qualification is expiring

  • Historical Record

    See what qualifications individuals held at a particular point in time if there’s ever an incident
