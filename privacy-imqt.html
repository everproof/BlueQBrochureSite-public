Privacy Collection Notice - IMQT.

This notice is provided to you by Clasity Pty Ltd ACN 168 177 259 (trading as BlueQ). BlueQ provides access to an online assessment questionnaire called I’m More Qualfiied Than Campaign (IMQT) served through a Facebook application. This notice sets out how BlueQ collects, uses, discloses and manages your personal information when you access and use the IMQT

Collection of personal information

BlueQ collects personal information directly from you when you use and submit your responses to the questions set out in the IMQT questionnaire or otherwise interact with BlueQ in relation to the IMQT Campaign. The type of personal information BlueQ collects includes:

  • your name;
  • your email address,
  • the qualifications you hold,
  • the industries you work in,
  • any responses to questions you submit as a result of your use of the IMQT Campaign; and
  • any other information you submit to BlueQ in using the IMQT Campaign or as a result of your other interactions or dealings with BlueQ.

BlueQ collects this personal information so that we can contact you to discuss the products and services of BlueQ or our commercial partners that may be of interest to you.

Use and disclosure of personal information

BlueQ will use your personal information to:

  • tell you which famous person you are more qualified than;
  • respond to any general queries you raise with us about our product offering;
  • provide you with information or other material we may consider to be relevant, useful or otherwise informative to you; and
  • contact you to discuss products or services of BlueQ or our commercial partners that may be of interest to you.

BlueQ does not currently disclose personal information to recipients located overseas or have established plans to do so.

BlueQ Privacy Policy

BlueQ has a privacy policy that is publically available on our website /privacy.html. The BlueQ privacy policy sets out details of how BlueQ will collect, store, manage, use and disclose personal information from you. The BlueQ privacy policy also sets out details of how you can access and seek correction of your personal information held by BlueQ. In addition, the BlueQ privacy policy sets out how you can complain to us about:

  • our handling of your personal information;
  • a breach of the Privacy Act or a registered code under the Privacy Act that binds BlueQ; and
  • how BlueQ will respond to and deal with a privacy complaint.
